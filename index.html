Sarah Paitz


I am currently working at Walgreens as a Pharmacy Technician while attending the Washington University Coding Bootcamp. After completing the Bootcamp, I will be a Full Stack Developer proficient in HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Express.js, React.js, Node.js, Progressive Web Apps, Database Theory, MongoDB, MySQL, Git, Python, Java, C#, PHP and more. I graduated from SIUE with a Bachelor's in Music and since I have pursued a music career for over 10 years, I have a unique perceptive of creativity and learning how to work efficiently within many groups.

Sarah Paitz (soprano) has performed in many operas and musical theater productions, her favorite roles including: Anne Trulove in The Rake's Progress, Gretel in Hansel and Gretel, Woman 2 in Songs for A New World and Cinderella in Into the Woods. She has participated in Union Avenue Opera's Crescendo Program and the Crittenden Opera program. Sarah's most recent accolade was becoming a finalist in the University of Kentucky's Alltech Vocal Competition.
